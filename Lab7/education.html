My Education

Hainesport School

My favorite class was my eighth grade English class because the teacher really cared and she taught us a lot.

I have lots of memories from this school as this was the school I went to from 1st to 8th grade but I will always remember the fun I had with my friends who I am still friends with to this day.

Rancocas Valley Regional High School

My favorite class was my engineering class because it was with the boys.

One of my favorite memories from high school is that I will always remember lunch out senior year with the boys.

Stevens Institute of Technology

My favorite class at Stevens so far is CS146 because I learned a lot and I didn't fall asleep in there.

The best memories I have from Stevens so far are the times I've spent with my roommates in our apartment and running together.
