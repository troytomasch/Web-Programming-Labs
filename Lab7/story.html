When I ran to Jersey City with my friends

One day for Cross Country, we had an on your own run. We call these runs OYO runs. This means that we can run at any time and don't need to wake up early in the morning. I typically do these runs with my teammates in my class and they are also the people I live with in my apartment.

I got up still kind of early that day with my friends as we all had class at 10 and wanted to get our run in before then. We all got up at around 8 am and met at the track office. No one else went with us but my roommates so we had a good sized group to have fun but not too big of a group.

We then ran all the way to Jersey City which wasn't very far. But then we cut through a hole in a fence and crossed a set of train tacks to run to this abandonded part of the railway. Unfortunately it had just rained really and we went down a tunnel before it got too muddy and we turned around and went back. One of my roommates had just gotten new shoes and while he didn't ruin them completely. They were not as clean as when he had started the run.
